Welcome to BGW

BGW is a diversified high-tech enterprise dedicated to building safety and energy consumption solutions. It strives to provide systematic solutions in terms of safety, health, efficiency, and energy conservation for property owners and designers. Leveraging advanced manufacturing technology and experience from Germany, BGW has developed a series of products tailored to the architectural characteristics of different regions around the world.

Get Started

Business Segments

BGW business consists of four departments: Design Consulting services, Solutions, Equipment and Software, and Starfield Institute

Design Consulting Services

BGW's professional design team can provide clients with specialized design consulting services, including standardization consulting and management in the field of engineering construction, full process consulting for building structures/electromechanical seismic resistance, energy-saving renovation consulting, design consulting services for cold storage and cold chain logistics, ice and snow and special refrigeration, petroleum storage and transportation, etc.

Solutions

BGW provides an integrated and systematic solution for design, product, and construction of building functionality. Mainly including: prefabricated comprehensive support hangers, rainwater drainage, installation of automatic production lines, seismic resistance of mechanical and electrical equipment, installation of flexible roofs, building sound insulation etc.

Equipment and Software

BGW, in collaboration with other research institutions, has developed mechanical testing machines that meet mainstream testing standards both domestically and internationally, filling a gap in this field in China; Multiple independently developed design and management software have solved the calculation problems in design, greatly improving work efficiency, and providing possibilities for BIM positive design through multi-disciplinary collaborative design.

Starfield Institute

Starfield Institute is committed to establishing global alumni resource links and cultivating industry leaders with deep knowledge and broad understanding. Taking domestic academic conferences, overseas visits, and technical exchanges as opportunities, we will unite representative enterprises from various nodes of the domestic and foreign industrial chains to help more corporate executives and technical personnel broaden their international horizons, enhance professional capabilities, integrate high-quality resources, improve the competitiveness of enterprises in domestic and international markets, and create more international business opportunities.
